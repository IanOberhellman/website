Mechanical Reverie

Scroll through the anatomy of a handcrafted movement. Every component glides into place to reveal a modern interpretation of a Swiss classic.

Scroll to assemble the watch

Mainplate

The foundation is milled from rhodium-coated brass, then brushed by hand to produce a satin bed that catches low-angle light. It anchors every jewel and bridge with micron-level discipline.

Barrel & Mainspring

The barrel stores the watch’s pulse. Our blued spring unwinds with consistent force, allowing a long reserve while preserving torque stability from first hour to last.

Gear Train

Skeletonized wheels with polished spokes transfer energy across jeweled pivots. Every tooth profile is tuned for low friction, creating the signature smooth sweep associated with fine movements.

Escapement

The lever escapement regulates time in precise beats. Angled pallets and a high-inertia balance maintain rhythm even through motion, delivering chronometric confidence.

Hands & Dial Ring

Dauphine hands are diamond-cut and heat-blued, floating above a minimalist chapter ring. The visual language is traditional, yet stripped to modern clarity.

Sapphire Crystal

A boxed sapphire crystal seals the composition with anti-reflective treatment on both faces. The movement now appears suspended, complete, and quietly dramatic.
